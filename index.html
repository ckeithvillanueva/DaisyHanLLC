About Daisy

Two decades of leading with purpose.

Daisy Han is a seasoned education and social innovation strategist with over 18 years of experience leading equity-centered transformation across K–12 systems, charter and Montessori ecosystems, higher education, and career-connected learning.

A trusted partner to districts, charter networks, foundations, and community organizations advancing racial equity and systems-level change.

Based in Makawao, Hawaii, Daisy brings a rare combination of on-the-ground classroom experience, nonprofit leadership, and philanthropic strategy. She founded and scaled Embracing Equity into a national organization serving 660,000+ students across 48 states — then brought those learnings back to the consulting world.

40 Under 40, Leaders of Color (2024) Harvard M.Ed., Educational Equity Montessori School Leadership (AMS) UC Berkeley, summa cum laude
